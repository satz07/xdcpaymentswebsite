FAQs

  • What is XDC Payments?

    XDC Payments is a blockchain-powered payment solution built on the XDC Network. It facilitates fast, secure, and cost-effective cross-border settlements using digital assets and ISO 20022-compliant messaging.

  • How does XDC Payments enable cross-border transactions?

    XDC Payments leverages blockchain technology for real-time settlements, utilizing stablecoins and digital assets to eliminate intermediaries, reduce transaction costs, and enhance speed.

  • What role does ISO 20022 play in the XDC Payments ecosystem?

    ISO 20022 is a global financial messaging standard that enhances interoperability between banks, fintechs, and payment providers. XDC Payments integrates ISO 20022 messaging formats such as pain.001 (payment initiation) and pacs.008 (final settlement) to streamline transactions.

  • How does XDC Payments support stablecoins?

    Stablecoins serve as a bridge between traditional and digital finance. Users can on-ramp fiat into stablecoins, transact across borders, and off-ramp into local fiat currencies seamlessly.

  • Can businesses and merchants integrate XDC Payments?

    Yes! Merchants can integrate XDC Payments to accept stablecoins and digital assets, allowing them to tap into a global customer base while benefiting from instant settlements and lower transaction fees.

  • What are the advantages of using XDC Payments over traditional payment systems?

    • Lower transaction costs due to blockchain efficiency.
    • Instant cross-border settlements without banking delays.
    • Interoperability with ISO 20022 ensures global compliance.
    • Stablecoin and fiat on/off-ramps provide seamless transactions.

  • How does XDC Payments ensure security and compliance?

    XDC Payments follows industry-standard security protocols, including cryptographic verification, AML/KYC compliance for regulated entities, and ISO 20022 message validation to meet global financial regulations.

  • Can XDC Payments be used for B2B transactions?

    Yes! Enterprises can use XDC Payments for cross-border trade, supply chain settlements, and corporate remittances with improved efficiency and reduced costs.

  • How does XDC Payments integrate with existing financial systems?

    XDC Payments offers APIs and SDKs for banks, fintechs, and merchants to integrate seamlessly with existing financial infrastructures while enabling blockchain-powered settlements.

  • Where can I learn more or get started with XDC Payments?

    To learn more about XDC Payments and how to integrate it into your business, visit XDC Network's official website or reach out to the XDC community for support.
