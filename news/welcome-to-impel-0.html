Financial Messaging

ISO 20022 is a universal financial industry message scheme, a multi part international standard prepared by ISO Technical Committee TC68 Financial Services.

Impel offers a powerful new way to send ISO 20022 compliant financial messaging over the XDC Network via an API that allows for complex data handling and high security. Benefits of ISO 20022

Flexibility: ISO 20022 can adapt more easily than current messaging standards, allowing for more responsiveness to changes in the economy, emerging technologies, and innovation.

Harmonization: Over 70 countries have already adopted ISO 20022, so messages will be harmonized with payment systems around the world.

Compliance and regulation: Richer data will make it easier for businesses to detect fraud and help target financial crime.

Resilience: ISO 20022 will be used across many payment systems, improving resilience by allowing re-routing of messages which reduces the impact of outages on users.

Enriched data: More data can be carried, enabling more detailed and better structured reference information.

Competition and innovation: More flexibility means more competition and innovation in the financial sector.

Straight-through processing: Less need for banks to make manual interventions, reducing delays for the end customer.

Analytics: Enriched data will improve analytics, allowing for improved decision making.

R3 Corda | XDC Network Bridge

R3â€™s platforms are geared towards regulated industries and utilize trust technology and confidential computing to facilitate business interactions between multiple parties.
